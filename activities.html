Our Activities

What is done by NUTCHE Enterprise.

🧪

Product Development

Formulating and testing nutrient-dense composite flours from local crops for households and vulnerable groups.

🌱

Farmer Partnerships

Sourcing maize, soya, groundnuts and sorghum directly from smallholder farmers, women and youth cooperatives.

🍲

Nutrition Education

Running community food demonstrations and training on food safety, quality and healthy diets.

📚

Research Collaboration

Partnering with universities and researchers to generate evidence that guides product and policy development.
